Docking

This fjord is traditionally rated as a winter harbour. The harbour is very secure.

Berth on the N of the outer wall or, if less than 34ft, it may be possible to use one of the slips in the small boat marina.

There is a good anchorage at the head of the fjord.

Water and provisions are available here, also diesel by arrangement with the boatyard.
